# Dashboard View Ideas

## Current Views

* ✅ Editor/Notes

* ✅ Todo List

* ✅ Calendar

* ✅ Home

## Future View Ideas

### 1. **Daily Journal**

A focused daily journaling space with:

* Date picker for browsing past entries

* Prompts/templates (gratitude, wins, learnings)

* Mood tracker

* Quick capture for thoughts throughout the day                                                          

### 2. **Habit Tracker**

Visual habit tracking:

* Grid/calendar view showing streaks

* Daily checkboxes for habits

* Statistics and insights (current streak, best streak, completion rate)

* Color-coded by habit category

### 3. **Focus Timer (Pomodoro)**

Productivity timer with:

* 25/5 minute intervals (customizable)

* Task selection from todo list

* Session history

* Daily/weekly focus time stats

### 4. **Quick Capture / Inbox**

Brain dump space for:

* Rapid note capture without opening full editor

* Voice-to-text notes

* Links/bookmarks to process later

* Ideas that need categorizing

### 5. **Goals Dashboard**

Goal tracking and progress:

* Long-term goals (quarterly, yearly)

* Progress bars and milestones

* Weekly/monthly reviews

* Goal breakdown into actionable tasks

### 6. **Reading List**

Book and article tracker:

* Currently reading

* Want to read queue

* Completed with ratings/notes

* Reading stats and insights

### 7. **Daily Overview**

Morning dashboard showing:

* Today's schedule/calendar

* Top 3 priorities

* Weather

* Quick stats (tasks completed, habits done)

* Inspirational quote

### 8. **Weekly Review**

Reflection and planning:

* Past week highlights

* Wins and challenges

* Next week planning

* Template-based prompts

### 9. **Project Board**

Kanban-style project management:

* Multiple projects

* Todo/In Progress/Done columns

* Project notes and resources

* Timeline view

### 10. **Mind Map / Brainstorm**

Visual thinking space:

* Node-based canvas

* Connect ideas visually

* Quick sketch area

* Export to notes

### 11. **Financial Tracker**

Simple budget overview:

* Monthly income/expenses

* Spending categories

* Savings goals

* Bill reminders

### 12. **Health & Wellness**

Personal health tracking:

* Water intake

* Exercise log

* Sleep tracker

* Meal planning

### 13. **Meeting Notes**

Structured meeting capture:

* Template with agenda/notes/action items

* Participant tracking

* Auto-link to calendar events

* Meeting archive

### 14. **Learning Log**

Knowledge management:

* TIL (Today I Learned) entries

* Skills you're developing

* Resources and courses

* Progress tracking

### 15. **Inspiration Board**

Visual collection:

* Images, quotes, links

* Pinterest-style grid

* Categories/tags

* Search and filter

## Implementation Priority

**High Priority (Most Useful Daily):**

1. Daily Journal

2. Habit Tracker

3. Quick Capture/Inbox

4. Daily Overview

**Medium Priority:** 5. Focus Timer 6. Goals Dashboard 7. Weekly Review

**Nice to Have:** 8. Project Board 9. Reading List 10. Meeting Notes

**Experimental:** 11. Mind Map 12. Financial Tracker 13. Health & Wellness 14. Learning Log 15. Inspiration Board

## Design Principles

* Each view should be toggleable from sidebar

* Views should work well independently or combined

* Keep the dotted journal aesthetic

* Maintain blue color scheme

* Focus on simplicity and speed

* Data should persist locally (vault folder)
